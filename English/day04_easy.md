## 📝 Common Interview Questions & Answers

### Q1: "Why do you want to be a Data Analyst?"
**Answer:** "I have always been interested in finding patterns in data. My finance background taught me how numbers tell stories. I enjoy cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing data to help businesses make better decisions."

### Q2: "Why did you choose Canada?"
**Answer:** "Canada has a strong data industry and a welcoming culture. I believe there are great opportunities here for someone with my skills. I am excited to contribute to the Canadian economy."

### Q3: "What are you looking for in a job?"
**Answer:** "I am looking for a role where I can use my analytical skills to solve real problems. I want to work in a team that values data-driven decision-making."

### Q4: "Tell me about a time you faced a challenge."
**Answer:** "During my internship, I was asked to create a report with incomplete data. I had to find alternative data sources and clean the data manually. In the end, the report was well received by my manager."

## 📖 Grammar Point: Question Words (Wh- Questions)
| Word | Use | Example |
|------|-----|---------|
| What | জিনিস/কাজ | "What kind of data did you analyze?" |
| Why | কারণ | "Why did you choose data analytics?" |
| Where | স্থান | "Where did you work before?" |
| When | সময় | "When did you start using SQL?" |
| How | পদ্ধতি | "How do you clean messy data?" |
| Which | নির্বাচন | "Which tools are you familiar with?" |

> **Practice Tip:** Listen to the question word. Your answer should match it. "Why..." = give a reason. "How..." = explain a method.

## 💡 Interview Tip
**Answer briefly but include an example.** Don't just say "I like data." Say "I like data because at my last job, I found a trend in sales that increased revenue by 10%."
