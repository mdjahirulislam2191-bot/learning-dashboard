## 📝 Key Strengths for Data Analyst Roles

### Technical Skills (প্রযুক্তিগত দক্ষতা)
| Skill | How to Say It |
|-------|--------------|
| Excel | "I am proficient in Excel, including pivot tables and VLOOKUP." |
| SQL | "I can write complex SQL queries to extract and join data." |
| Python | "I have basic to intermediate Python skills, especially for data cleaning." |
| Tableau/Power BI | "I have experience creating dashboards in Power BI." |
| Statistics | "I understand statistical concepts like regression and hypothesis testing." |

### Soft Skills (সফট স্কিল)
| Skill | How to Say It |
|-------|--------------|
| Communication | "I can explain technical findings to non-technical people." |
| Problem-solving | "I enjoy finding creative solutions to data problems." |
| Teamwork | "I work well in cross-functional teams." |
| Time management | "I can manage multiple projects and meet deadlines." |

## 📖 Grammar Point: "I am" + Adjective / "I have" + Noun

**"I am" + adjective:**
- I am organized.
- I am detail-oriented.
- I am analytical.
- I am hardworking.

**"I have" + noun:**
- I have strong analytical skills.
- I have experience with SQL.
- I have a background in finance.
- I have excellent communication skills.

## 📝 Sample Answer: "What are your strengths?"
**Answer:** "My greatest strength is my ability to combine finance knowledge with data analysis. I understand both the business side and the technical side. I am also very detail-oriented — I check my work carefully to ensure accuracy. Additionally, I am comfortable communicating findings to managers who may not be technical."

## 💡 Interview Tip
**Choose strengths that are relevant to the job.** For Data Analyst roles, focus on: analytical thinking, attention to detail, technical skills (Excel/SQL), and business acumen. Always give a short example after stating your strength.
