# Day 1: Greetings & Introductions (শুভেচ্ছা ও পরিচয়)

## 🔤 Daily Vocabulary (দৈনিক শব্দভাণ্ডার)
| English | Bangla | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Hello / Hi | হ্যালো / হাই | "Hello, my name is Jahirul." |
| Good morning | সুপ্রভাত | "Good morning, thank you for having me." |
| Nice to meet you | আপনার সাথে দেখা হয়ে ভালো লাগলো | "Nice to meet you, Mr. Smith." |
| How are you? | আপনি কেমন আছেন? | "How are you today?" |
| I am fine | আমি ভালো আছি | "I am fine, thank you." |
| My name is... | আমার নাম... | "My name is Jahirul Islam." |
| I am from... | আমি ... থেকে এসেছি | "I am originally from Bangladesh." |
| Currently | বর্তমানে | "I am currently living in Toronto." |

## 📝 Conversation Practice (কথোপকথন অনুশীলন)

### Dialogue 1: First Meeting
**Interviewer:** "Good morning. Please come in."
**You:** "Good morning. Thank you for having me today."
**Interviewer:** "How are you doing?"
**You:** "I am doing well, thank you. And you?"
**Interviewer:** "I'm great. So, tell me a little about yourself."

### Dialogue 2: On the Phone
**Receptionist:** "Hello, this is ABC Corp. How may I help you?"
**You:** "Hello, my name is Jahirul Islam. I am calling about the Data Analyst position."
**Receptionist:** "One moment, please. I'll connect you."

## 📖 Grammar Point: Present Simple with "To Be"
| Subject | Verb | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| I | am | I am a Data Analyst. |
| You | are | You are the hiring manager. |
| He/She | is | She is the team lead. |
| We | are | We are from the analytics team. |
| They | are | They are looking for analysts. |

## 💡 Interview Tip
**Eye contact and a firm handshake matter more than perfect English.** Practice saying your name clearly. In Canadian interviews, a warm smile and saying "Thank you for this opportunity" creates a strong first impression.

## 🏠 Homework (বাড়ির কাজ)
1. Write a 30-second introduction about yourself in English.
2. Practice saying these sentences out loud 5 times:
   - "Good morning. My name is Jahirul Islam."
   - "I am a finance graduate looking for Data Analyst roles."
   - "I am currently living in Canada."
