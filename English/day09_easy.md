## 📝 3 STAR Stories Every Data Analyst Should Prepare

### Story 1: A Data Cleaning Challenge
**Question:** "Tell me about a time you worked with messy data."

**S:** "In my internship, I was given a customer database with over 50,000 records. The data had many duplicates, missing values, and inconsistent formats."

**T:** "I needed to clean the data so the marketing team could run a campaign."

**A:** "I used Excel to remove duplicates and standardize formats. For missing values, I researched the correct information. I documented every change I made."

**R:** "The cleaned dataset was 100% accurate. The marketing campaign achieved a 15% higher response rate than previous campaigns."

### Story 2: A Dashboard You Created
**Question:** "Have you ever created a dashboard or report?"

**S:** "At my previous job, management wanted a way to track monthly sales performance visually."

**T:** "I was tasked with creating an interactive dashboard."

**A:** "I used Power BI to connect to the sales database. I designed charts showing revenue, customer count, and product performance by region. I added filters so managers could view specific time periods."

**R:** "The dashboard was adopted by the entire sales team. It saved 5 hours of manual reporting each week and helped identify a $30,000 cost-saving opportunity."

### Story 3: A Data-Driven Decision
**Question:** "Tell me about a time your analysis helped make a decision."

**S:** "Our company was considering which product to discontinue."

**T:** "My task was to analyze sales data for our three lowest-performing products."

**A:** "I calculated profit margins, customer return rates, and sales trends over 12 months. I presented my analysis in a clear comparison table."

**R:** "Based on my analysis, the company discontinued the least profitable product and redirected resources. This increased overall profit by 8% in the next quarter."

## 📖 Grammar Point: Connecting Words for Stories
| Word | Use | Example |
|------|-----|---------|
| First | প্রথমে | "First, I collected the data." |
| Then | তারপর | "Then, I cleaned it." |
| After that | এরপর | "After that, I created charts." |
| Finally | শেষে | "Finally, I presented my findings." |
| As a result | ফলে | "As a result, the team saved time." |
| Because of this | এর কারণে | "Because of this, profits increased." |

## 💡 Interview Tip
**Practice telling your stories WITHOUT looking at notes.** The more natural you sound, the more confident you appear. Record yourself and listen back.
