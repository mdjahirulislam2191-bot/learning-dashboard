## 📝 10 Final Tips for Interview Success

### Before the Interview (ইন্টারভিউর আগে)
1. **Research the company** — Know their products, values, and recent news
2. **Review the job description** — Match your skills to their requirements
3. **Prepare 3 STAR stories** — Practice until they feel natural
4. **Prepare 3 questions to ask** — Shows genuine interest
5. **Plan your outfit** — Dress one level above the company dress code

### During the Interview (ইন্টারভিউর সময়)
6. **Smile and make eye contact** — Even on video calls, look at the camera
7. **Speak slowly and clearly** — Rushing makes you sound nervous
8. **Use the STAR method** — Structure your answers
9. **Be honest** — If you don't know, say so politely

### After the Interview (ইন্টারভিউর পরে)
10. **Send a thank-you email** — Within 2 hours. Make it specific.

## 📖 Final Grammar Review: 10 Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Correction |
|---------|------------|
| "I am work in..." | "I **work** in..." / "I am **working** in..." |
| "I have 2 years experiences" | "I have 2 years **of experience**" |
| "I am from Bangladesh born" | "I **was born** in Bangladesh" |
| "I am very like data" | "I **really like** data" |
| "He go to office" | "He **goes** to the office" |
| "I didn't went" | "I didn't **go**" |
| "She don't like" | "She **doesn't** like" |
| "More better" | "**Better**" (not more better) |
| "The data show that" | "The data **shows** that" |
| "I am agree" | "I **agree**" |
