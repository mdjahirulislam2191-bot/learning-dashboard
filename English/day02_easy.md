## 📝 Conversation Practice (কথোপকথন অনুশীলন)

### Dialogue: Talking About Education
**Interviewer:** "Can you tell me about your educational background?"
**You:** "Certainly. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Finance from the University of Dhaka. My coursework included statistics, econometrics, and financial modeling."
**Interviewer:** "Interesting. Did you take any data-related courses?"
**You:** "Yes. I took courses in Business Statistics, Database Management, and Quantitative Methods. I also completed a Data Analytics certificate online."

### Key Phrases for Education Talks:
- "I graduated from..."
- "I hold a degree in..."
- "During my studies, I focused on..."
- "I completed additional training in..."
- "My education gave me a strong foundation in..."

## 📖 Grammar Point: Past Simple Tense
Use past simple to talk about your education.

| Subject | Past Verb | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| I | studied | I studied Finance at university. |
| I | completed | I completed my degree in 2020. |
| I | took | I took several statistics courses. |
| I | learned | I learned SQL during my internship. |
| I | graduated | I graduated with honors. |

**Negative form:** "I did not study computer science formally."

## 💡 Interview Tip
When talking about your finance background for a Data Analyst role, **connect the dots for the interviewer**. Say things like:
- "My finance background taught me how to work with numbers and draw insights."
- "I realized I enjoyed analyzing data more than just managing accounts."
