## 📝 Email Templates for Job Search

### 1. Application Follow-up Email
**Subject:** Follow-up on Data Analyst Application - Jahirul Islam

**Body:**
Dear [Hiring Manager Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to follow up on my application for the Data Analyst position I submitted on [date]. I am very interested in this opportunity and would love to discuss how my background in finance and data analysis can contribute to your team.

Please let me know if you need any additional information from me.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Best regards,
Jahirul Islam
[Phone Number]
[Email]

### 2. Thank You Email After Interview
**Subject:** Thank You - Data Analyst Interview - Jahirul Islam

**Body:**
Dear [Interviewer Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to meet with me today. I really enjoyed learning more about the Data Analyst role and your company's work.

I was particularly excited about the data visualization projects you mentioned. I believe my experience with Power BI and my finance background would make me a strong contributor to your analytics team.

Please feel free to reach out if you have any further questions.

Thank you again for this opportunity.

Warm regards,
Jahirul Islam

### 3. Request for Informational Interview
**Subject:** Request for Informational Interview - Data Analytics

**Body:**
Dear [Name],

My name is Jahirul Islam. I am a finance graduate currently transitioning into Data Analytics in Canada. I came across your profile on LinkedIn and was impressed by your career journey.

Would you be available for a brief 15-minute chat next week? I would love to hear your advice on breaking into the data analytics field in Canada.

Thank you for your time.

Best regards,
Jahirul Islam

## 📖 Grammar Point: Formal vs. Informal Email Language

| Informal | Formal |
|----------|--------|
| "Hey John" | "Dear Mr. Smith" |
| "Thanks!" | "Thank you very much." |
| "I want..." | "I would like to..." |
| "Can you..." | "Would you be able to..." |
| "Send me..." | "Please send me..." |
| "Talk soon" | "I look forward to hearing from you." |

## 💡 Interview Tip
**Always send a thank-you email within 24 hours** after an interview. It shows professionalism and keeps you top of mind. Keep it short, specific, and genuine.
