## 📝 Conversation: Talking About Daily Work

### Dialogue
**Interviewer:** "Can you describe a typical day at your last job?"
**You:** "Sure. I usually started my day by checking emails and reviewing any urgent requests. Then I would check the data pipeline to make sure everything was running smoothly. The afternoons were spent on deeper analysis — preparing reports in Excel or running SQL queries. I always ended the day by documenting my work and planning the next day's tasks."

### Useful Phrases for Describing Routines:
- "I usually start my day by..."
- "First, I check..."
- "Then, I move on to..."
- "In the afternoon, I focus on..."
- "I always make sure to..."
- "Before finishing, I..."

## 🕐 Time Expressions (সময় প্রকাশ)
| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| Usually | সাধারণত | "I usually work with Excel." |
| Often | প্রায়ই | "I often run SQL queries." |
| Sometimes | মাঝে মাঝে | "Sometimes I present findings to managers." |
| Rarely | খুব কমই | "I rarely miss a deadline." |
| Every day | প্রতিদিন | "I update dashboards every day." |
| Once a week | সপ্তাহে একবার | "I have team meetings once a week." |

## 📖 Grammar Point: Present Simple for Routines
Use **present simple** to describe regular actions.

| Subject | Verb | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| I | analyze | "I analyze data every morning." |
| I | prepare | "I prepare weekly reports." |
| He/She | analyzes | "She analyzes customer data." |
| He/She | prepares | "He prepares monthly summaries." |

**With adverbs of frequency:** Adverb goes before the main verb.
- "I **always** check my data for errors."
- "I **usually** finish my reports by Friday."
- "I **never** submit work without reviewing it."

## 💡 Interview Tip
When asked about your work habits, **show you are organized and reliable**. Data analyst roles require consistency and accuracy. Use phrases like:
- "I keep a detailed log of my analyses."
- "I double-check my work before submitting."
- "I am very organized with my files and code."
