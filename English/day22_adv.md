### 🎯 Part 3: Technical Questions (10 minutes)

**Q1:** "What is the difference between a Primary Key and a Foreign Key?"
**Answer:** "A Primary Key uniquely identifies each row in a table. A Foreign Key is a field in one table that links to the Primary Key of another table. For example, in a Customers table, CustomerID is the Primary Key. In an Orders table, CustomerID is a Foreign Key that connects each order to a customer."

**Q2:** "What is a LEFT JOIN in SQL? Give an example."
**Answer:** "A LEFT JOIN returns all rows from the left table and matching rows from the right table. If there is no match, the result shows NULL. For example: SELECT Customers.Name, Orders.OrderID FROM Customers LEFT JOIN Orders ON Customers.ID = Orders.CustomerID. This returns all customers, even those without orders."

**Q3:** "Explain the difference between mean and median. When would you use one over the other?"
**Answer:** "Mean is the average of all values. Median is the middle value when data is sorted. I would use median when data has outliers — for example, if one executive earns $1M and everyone else earns $50K, the mean salary would be misleading. The median would give a better picture of a typical salary."

| Category | Questions |
|----------|-----------|
| Personal | Tell me about yourself. Why data analytics? What are your strengths/weaknesses? |
| Behavioral | Tell me about a time you... solved a problem / worked in a team / handled pressure / made a mistake / led a project |
| Technical | What SQL commands do you use? How do you clean data? Explain a statistical concept. What is a pivot table? |
| Business | How would you measure success? How do you prioritize tasks? How do you present findings? |

## 💡 Final Interview Tips
1. **Arrive 10 minutes early** (for virtual: test your camera and audio)
2. **Dress professionally** even for video calls
3. **Have water nearby** — your mouth gets dry when nervous
4. **Take a breath before answering** — it shows confidence
5. **SMILE** — enthusiasm matters as much as skills
6. **Send a thank-you email** within 2 hours of the interview
